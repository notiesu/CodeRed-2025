Welcome to MathsVoice

MathsVoice is designed to make STEM education accessible for everyone, including people with learning disabilities and visual impairments. Our AI-powered platform converts math and science content into clear, narrated audio, making learning inclusive and engaging.

About MathsVoice

MathsVoice uses advanced AI to convert math and science documents into spoken word, helping students with dyslexia, ADHD, visual impairments, and other learning differences. Our platform supports multiple formats, offers customizable narration, and is built with accessibility in mind.

  • Audio narration for math and science content
  • Accessible design for screen readers and keyboard navigation
  • Customizable voices and playback speed
  • Easy document upload and processing
